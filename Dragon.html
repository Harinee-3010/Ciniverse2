Dragon

Story:

Dragon (2025), directed by Ashwath Marimuthu and co-written with Pradeep Ranganathan, follows Ragavan “Dragon” Dhanapal, a once-promising college student whose life spirals after a painful breakup. Consumed by ego and desperation, he fakes his credentials to land a high-paying job, only to be forced back to college when his deceit is discovered. Tasked with clearing 48 arrears, Ragavan’s journey to redemption takes an emotional turn as he reunites with his ex-girlfriend Keerthi, now a faculty member. Set in a youthful, vibrant backdrop, the film blends humor, romance, and a strong message about self-worth and second chances. With lively music by Leon James and striking visuals, Dragon struck a chord with audiences, becoming one of the top-grossing Tamil films of 2025 and later streaming on Netflix in multiple languages.
