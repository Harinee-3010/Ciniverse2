Beauty And The Beast

Story:

Beauty and the Beast (2017) tells the story of Belle, a smart and kind young woman who is taken prisoner by a Beast in his enchanted castle. Though frightened at first, she slowly befriends the castle’s magical staff and begins to see beyond the Beast’s frightening appearance. As they spend time together, Belle discovers the Beast’s gentle and caring side. Meanwhile, her father tries to rescue her, and a vain hunter named Gaston plans to kill the Beast out of jealousy. Belle’s love eventually breaks the curse that had turned the prince into a beast. The castle and its inhabitants return to their human forms. The film ends with a grand celebration of love and transformation.
