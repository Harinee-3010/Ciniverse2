Story:

Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker (2019), directed by J.J. Abrams, is the epic conclusion to the nine-part Skywalker saga. Set about a year after The Last Jedi, the film finds Rey (Daisy Ridley), Finn, and Poe Dameron leading the Resistance against the First Order, now under Kylo Ren and the mysterious influence of a resurrected Emperor Palpatine. Through a galaxy-spanning quest involving Sith wayfinders and clues on worlds like Pasaana, Kijimi, and Endor, they uncover Palpatine’s plan to deploy a devastating “Final Order” fleet from Exegol. Rey confronts her dark lineage—revealed as Palpatine’s granddaughter—and duels Kylo, who ultimately redeems himself and becomes Ben Solo. In the climactic battle on Exegol, Rey, aided by the voices of past Jedi and Ben’s sacrifice, defeats Palpatine, bringing an end to the Sith and restoring hope. The film closes with Rey adopting the name “Rey Skywalker,” returning to Tatooine to lay to rest Luke and Leia’s lightsabers and honor the legacy of her Jedi mentors .
